Given your budget of $800, I've created a 7-day trip plan from Hyderabad to Manali that incorporates historical sightseeing, cultural experiences, and delicious food. Since the budget is limited, I've suggested affordable options for transportation, accommodation, and activities.

**Day 1: Hyderabad to Delhi**

* Flight: Book a flight from Hyderabad to Delhi using a budget airline like IndiGo or SpiceJet. You can expect to pay around $100-$150 for a one-way ticket. (Booking platform: MakeMyTrip, Cleartrip, or Skyscanner)
* Transportation: From Delhi airport, take a taxi or metro to your hotel. (Cost: $10-$20)
* Hotel: Check-in at a budget hotel like Hotel Sun International or similar. (Cost: $20-$30 per night)
* Food: Try some delicious street food at Chandni Chowk or Connaught Place. (Cost: $5-$10 per meal)

**Day 2: Delhi to Manali**

* Bus: Book a semi-sleeper bus from Delhi to Manali using a bus booking platform like RedBus or MakeMyTrip. (Cost: $20-$30)
* Transportation: The bus journey takes around 12-14 hours, depending on traffic and road conditions.
* Hotel: Check-in at a budget hotel like Hotel Snow View or similar in Manali. (Cost: $25-$35 per night)
* Food: Try some local cuisine like momos, thukpa, or Tibetan tea at a restaurant like Cafe 1947 or Dylan's Toasted and Roasted Coffee House. (Cost: $10-$20 per meal)

**Day 3: Manali**

* Activity: Visit the Hadimba Devi Temple, a historic wooden temple dedicated to Goddess Hadimba. (Entry fee: $2-$3)
* Activity: Explore the Manu Temple, a beautiful temple dedicated to Sage Manu. (Entry fee: $2-$3)
* Food: Try some local snacks like bhel puri, sev puri, or chaat items at a street food stall. (Cost: $5-$10 per snack)
* Cultural Experience: Visit the Tibetan Monastery, a beautiful monastery with stunning architecture and a peaceful atmosphere. (Entry fee: free, but donations are welcome)

**Day 4: Manali**

* Activity: Take a day trip to Solang Valley, a beautiful valley with stunning views of the Himalayas. (Cost: $10-$20 per person, including transportation and activities)
* Activity: Visit the Jogini Falls, a beautiful waterfall surrounded by lush green forests. (Entry fee: $2-$3)
* Food: Try some local cuisine like trout fish or riverbed camping at a restaurant like The Johnson Hotel or similar. (Cost: $20-$30 per meal)
* Cultural Experience: Attend a traditional folk dance performance at a local cultural center. (Entry fee: $5-$10 per person)

**Day 5: Manali**

* Activity: Visit the Vashisht Hot Water Springs, a natural hot spring with therapeutic properties. (Entry fee: $2-$3)
* Activity: Explore the Old Manali village, a charming village with traditional houses and scenic views. (Entry fee: free)
* Food: Try some local snacks like popcorn, peanuts, or chana masala at a street food stall. (Cost: $5-$10 per snack)
* Cultural Experience: Visit the Manali Museum, a museum showcasing the history and culture of the region. (Entry fee: $2-$3)

**Day 6: Manali**

* Activity: Take a day trip to Rohtang Pass, a high-altitude pass with stunning views of the Himalayas. (Cost: $20-$30 per person, including transportation and activities)
* Activity: Visit the Gulaba village, a charming village with traditional houses and scenic views. (Entry fee: free)
* Food: Try some local cuisine like rajma chawal or kadhi chawal at a restaurant like The Himalayan Kitchen or similar. (Cost: $15-$25 per meal)
* Cultural Experience: Attend a yoga or meditation session at a local wellness center. (Entry fee: $10-$20 per person)

**Day 7: Manali to Hyderabad**

* Bus: Book a semi-sleeper bus from Manali to Delhi using a bus booking platform like RedBus or MakeMyTrip. (Cost: $20-$30)
* Flight: Book a flight from Delhi to Hyderabad using a budget airline like IndiGo or SpiceJet. (Cost: $100-$150)
* Transportation: From Delhi airport, take a taxi or metro to your destination. (Cost: $10-$20)

**Budget Breakdown:**

* Flights: $200-$300 (round-trip)
* Bus tickets: $40-$60 (round-trip)
* Accommodation: $150-$250 (avg. $20-$35 per night)
* Food: $150-$250 (avg. $20-$35 per day)
* Activities: $50-$100 (avg. $10-$20 per activity)
* Transportation: $20-$40 (avg. $5-$10 per day)
* Total: $700-$800
