Given your budget of $3500 for a trip from Devarakonda to Dubai, I've crafted a 7-day itinerary that balances historical sightseeing, cultural experiences, and delicious food, while also considering transportation, accommodation, and other travel essentials.

### Day 1: Travel from Devarakonda to Hyderabad

- **Morning:** Start your journey from Devarakonda to Hyderabad. The best mode of transport would be by bus or taxi, as there are no direct trains or flights. The journey takes approximately 4-5 hours by road.
- **Afternoon:** Check into a budget-friendly hotel in Hyderabad for a night's stay. You can use booking platforms like OYO Rooms or Booking.com for affordable options.
- **Evening:** Explore the local cuisine and culture of Hyderabad. Visit the Charminar and try the famous Hyderabadi biryani at one of the local restaurants.

### Day 2: Flight from Hyderabad to Dubai

- **Morning:** After breakfast, head to the Rajiv Gandhi International Airport (HYD) for your flight to Dubai. You can book your flight through travel websites like Expedia, Skyscanner, or directly from airlines like Emirates or Indigo, which often have affordable options.
- **Afternoon:** Board your flight. The journey from Hyderabad to Dubai is about 3 hours.
- **Evening:** Upon arrival at Dubai International Airport (DXB), proceed to immigration and then collect your baggage. You can use the Dubai Metro or a taxi to reach your hotel.
- **Night:** Check into a hotel in Dubai. For budget-friendly options, consider hotels like the Premier Inn Dubai Al Jaddaf or similar, which can be booked through Booking.com or Hotels.com.

### Day 3: Dubai City Tour

- **Morning:** Start your day with a delicious Arabic breakfast at a local caf√©.
- **9:00 AM:** Begin your Dubai city tour. Visit the Jumeirah Mosque, one of the most beautiful mosques in Dubai, and then head to the Al Fahidi Historical Neighbourhood to experience the traditional Arabic architecture.
- **1:00 PM:** Have lunch at a local restaurant in the historical neighborhood. Try some traditional Emirati dishes like Machboos or Shawarma.
- **2:30 PM:** Visit the Dubai Museum to learn about the history and culture of Dubai.
- **5:00 PM:** Take a stroll along the Dubai Creek, a historic area that showcases the traditional side of Dubai.
- **Evening:** Enjoy a Dhow Cruise in Dubai Creek with dinner. This will give you a beautiful view of the city from the water.

### Day 4: Modern Dubai

- **9:00 AM:** Start your day with a visit to the iconic Burj Khalifa, the tallest building in the world. Book your tickets in advance through the official Burj Khalifa website or a travel website like GetYourGuide.
- **11:00 AM:** After visiting the Burj Khalifa, walk through the Dubai Mall, one of the largest shopping malls in the world. You can enjoy the Dubai Fountain Show outside the mall.
- **1:00 PM:** Have lunch at one of the many restaurants in the mall.
- **2:30 PM:** Visit the Miracle Garden, a floral wonderland with stunning flower arrangements and designs.
- **5:00 PM:** Head to the Palm Jumeirah and visit the Atlantis, The Palm hotel. Even if you're not staying here, the hotel's lobby and surroundings are worth exploring.
- **Evening:** Enjoy dinner at one of the many international restaurants on the Palm.

### Day 5: Desert Safari

- **8:00 AM:** Have an early breakfast and then get ready for a Desert Safari. Book your safari through a travel website like Viator or GetYourGuide. The tour typically includes dune bashing, camel riding, and a visit to a Bedouin camp where you can enjoy Arabic coffee and dates.
- **7:00 PM:** Enjoy a BBQ dinner in the desert under the stars, followed by traditional belly dance performances.
- **10:00 PM:** Return to your hotel in Dubai.

### Day 6: Shopping and Leisure

- **Morning:** Spend your day shopping at one of Dubai's many malls, such as the Mall of the Emirates or the Ibn Battuta Mall. These malls offer a mix of luxury brands and affordable shopping, along with entertainment options like skiing at Ski Dubai in the Mall of the Emirates.
- **Evening:** If you're interested, you can visit the Global Village, a cultural and entertainment park that offers a glimpse into the culture of various countries around the world.

### Day 7: Departure

- **Morning:** Spend your last few hours in Dubai shopping for souvenirs or visiting any last-minute sights.
- **Afternoon:** Check out of your hotel and head to the Dubai International Airport for your return flight to Hyderabad.
- **Evening:** Board your flight back home, bringing back memories of your trip to Dubai.

### Tips:
- Book your flights and hotels well in advance to get the best rates.
- Use travel websites and booking platforms to compare prices and find deals.
- Consider purchasing a Dubai Tourist SIM card for data and local calls.
- Stay hydrated, especially during the desert safari.
- Respect local customs and dress modestly when visiting mosques or traditional areas.
