What a fantastic trip you're planning! With a budget of $30,000, I've created a 12-day itinerary for you to explore the beautiful island of Bali, Indonesia, from Hyderabad, India. This trip plan includes a mix of historical sightseeing, cultural experiences, and delicious food, along with transportation, accommodation, and activity suggestions.

**Day 1: Hyderabad to Bali**

* Flight: Hyderabad (HYD) to Bali (DPS) with a layover in Kuala Lumpur (KUL) or Singapore (SIN)
* Airlines: Malaysia Airlines, Singapore Airlines, or Thai Airways
* Cost: Approximately $800 - $1,200 per person (depending on the airline and time of booking)
* Flight duration: Around 10 hours (including layover)

**Day 2-3: Arrival in Bali and South Kuta Exploration**

* Hotel: The Anvaya Beach Resorts Bali (5-star) - $200 per night
* Location: South Kuta, close to Kuta Beach, Seminyak, and Legian
* Activities:
	+ Visit the famous Uluwatu Temple and watch the Kecak fire dance
	+ Explore the vibrant streets of South Kuta, Seminyak, and Legian
	+ Enjoy water sports, such as surfing or paddleboarding, at Kuta Beach
* Food: Try local cuisine at restaurants like Naughty Nuri's, The Beach House, or Made's Warung

**Day 4-5: Ubud Cultural Experience**

* Hotel: Four Seasons Resort Bali at Sayan (5-star) - $300 per night
* Location: Ubud, surrounded by lush green forests and rice fields
* Activities:
	+ Visit the Ubud Monkey Forest and explore the town's art market
	+ Watch a traditional Balinese dance performance at the Ubud Royal Palace
	+ Take a cooking class and learn about local ingredients and recipes
* Food: Enjoy local delicacies at restaurants like Naughty Nuri's, The Bridges, or Locavore

**Day 6-7: North Bali Adventure**

* Hotel: The Menjangan (5-star) - $250 per night
* Location: North Bali, near the West Bali National Park
* Activities:
	+ Take a boat tour to see dolphins in their natural habitat
	+ Explore the West Bali National Park and spot exotic birds and wildlife
	+ Visit the stunning Gitgit Waterfall and swim in the natural pools
* Food: Try fresh seafood at local restaurants like The Beach House or The Dive

**Day 8-9: East Bali Exploration**

* Hotel: Alila Manggis (5-star) - $200 per night
* Location: East Bali, near the town of Candidasa
* Activities:
	+ Visit the ancient Tirta Gangga water palace and explore the surrounding countryside
	+ Take a boat tour to see the coral reefs and marine life at Amed Beach
	+ Explore the traditional villages and markets of East Bali
* Food: Enjoy local cuisine at restaurants like The Water Palace or The Village

**Day 10-12: Nusa Dua and Departure**

* Hotel: The Mulia, Nusa Dua, Bali (5-star) - $300 per night
* Location: Nusa Dua, a luxurious beach resort area
* Activities:
	+ Relax on the beautiful beaches of Nusa Dua
	+ Take a snorkeling or diving trip to the nearby coral reefs
	+ Visit the nearby Uluwatu Temple and watch the sunset
* Food: Enjoy fine dining at restaurants like The Mulia's Signature Restaurant or The Beach House
