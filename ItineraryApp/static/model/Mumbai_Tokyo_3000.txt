**Day 1: Arrival in Tokyo**
* Arrive at Narita or Haneda airport in Tokyo, Japan (Cost: $600-$800 for flights from Mumbai)
* Check-in to a hotel in the Shinjuku district, such as [Hotel Gajoen Tokyo](https://www.hotelgajoen-tokyo.com/) (Cost: $100-$150 per night)
* Visit the nearby Shinjuku Gyoen National Garden for a relaxing stroll (Cost: $2-$3)
* Explore the Shinjuku district, known for its vibrant nightlife, restaurants, and shopping (Cost: $20-$50 for food and drinks)

**Day 2: Tokyo Landmarks**
* Visit the famous Tsukiji Outer Market for a sushi breakfast (Cost: $20-$30)
* Take the subway to the Shibuya district and visit the Shibuya Crossing (Cost: $2-$3 for subway fare)
* Walk to the nearby Meiji Shrine, a serene Shinto shrine (Cost: free)
* Visit the Tokyo Tower for panoramic views of the city (Cost: $15-$20)

**Day 3: Cultural Experiences**
* Attend a traditional tea ceremony at the [Happo-en Garden](https://www.happo-en.com/) (Cost: $20-$30)
* Visit the Ghibli Museum, dedicated to the works of Studio Ghibli (Cost: $20-$25)
* Explore the Asakusa district, one of the oldest areas of Tokyo (Cost: $10-$20 for food and snacks)
* Visit the Senso-ji Temple, a colorful and historic Buddhist temple (Cost: free)

**Day 4: Historical Sightseeing**
* Visit the Imperial Palace, the main residence of the Emperor of Japan (Cost: free)
* Take a stroll through the beautiful East Garden (Cost: free)
* Visit the Tokyo National Museum, which houses a vast collection of Japanese art and artifacts (Cost: $5-$10)
* Explore the Akihabara district, known for its electronics and anime culture (Cost: $20-$50 for food and shopping)

**Day 5: Day Trip to Nikko**
* Take a day trip to Nikko, a UNESCO World Heritage site located about two hours away from Tokyo (Cost: $50-$100 for transportation and entrance fees)
* Visit the Toshogu Shrine, a ornate Shinto shrine (Cost: $10-$20)
* Explore the Rinno-ji Temple, a historic Buddhist temple (Cost: $5-$10)
* Return to Tokyo in the evening and enjoy a traditional Japanese dinner (Cost: $30-$50)

**Day 6: Departure from Tokyo**
* Spend the morning shopping for last-minute souvenirs or visiting any last-minute sights (Cost: $20-$50)
* Depart for the airport and fly back to Mumbai (Cost: $600-$800 for flights)

**Budget Breakdown:**
* Flights from Mumbai to Tokyo: $600-$800
* Accommodation (5 nights): $500-$750
* Food and drink: $300-$500
* Transportation: $50-$100
* Entrance fees and activities: $150-$250
* Total: $1600-$2500
